To stroke the double-quote glyph:
Save the context.
Turn right.
Move 1/2 square.
Turn left.
Move 2-1/2 squares.
Stroke 1-1/2 squares.
Reset the context.
Turn right.
Move 1-1/2 squares.
Turn left.
Move 2-1/2 squares.
Stroke 1-1/2 squares.
Restore the context.
