
To stroke the big-r glyph:
Save the context.
Stroke 4 squares.
Turn right.
Stroke 1 square.
Turn right 1/8.
Stroke 1 square slantways.
Turn right.
Stroke 1 square slantways.
Turn left 1/8.
Turn left 7/96.
Stroke 7/2 square.
Restore the context.

To stroke the big-s glyph:
Save the context.
Move 1 square.
Turn around.
Turn left 1/8.
Stroke 1 square slantways.
