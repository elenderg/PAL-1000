To stroke the big-x glyph:
Save the context.
Stroke 1 square.
Turn right 1/8.
Stroke 2 squares slantways.
Turn left 1/8.
Stroke 1 square.
Reset the context.
Turn right.
Move 2 squares.
Turn left.
Stroke 1 square.
Turn left 1/8.
Stroke 2 squares slantways.
Turn right 1/8.
Stroke 1 square.
Restore the context.
