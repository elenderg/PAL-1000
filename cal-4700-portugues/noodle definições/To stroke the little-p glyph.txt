To stroke the little-p glyph:
Save the context.
Move 3 squares.
Turn around.
Stroke 4 squares.
Turn around.
Move 3 squares.
Turn right 1/8.
Stroke 1 square slantways.
Turn right.
Stroke 1 square slantways.
Turn right 1/8.
Stroke 1 square.
Turn right 1/8.
Stroke 1 square slantways.
Turn right.
Stroke 1 square slantways.
Restore the context.
