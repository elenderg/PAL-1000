To stroke the big-k glyph:
Save the context.
Stroke 4 squares.
Turn around.
Move 2 squares.
Turn left.
Stroke 1 square.
Save the context.
Turn left 1/8.
Stroke 1 square slantways.
Turn left 1/8.
Stroke 1 square.
Restore the context.
Turn right 1/8.
Stroke 1 square slantways.
Turn right 1/8.
Stroke 1 square.
Restore the context.
