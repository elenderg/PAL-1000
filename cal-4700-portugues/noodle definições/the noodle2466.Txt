
To stroke the little-r glyph:
Save the context.
Stroke 3 squares.
Turn around.
Move 1 square.
Turn left 3/8.
Stroke 1 square slantways.
Turn right.
Stroke 1 square slantways.
Restore the context.

To stroke the little-s glyph:
Save the context.
Move 1 square.
Turn right 3/8.
Stroke 1 square slantways.
Turn left.
Stroke 1 square slantways.
Reset the context.
Move 2 squares.
