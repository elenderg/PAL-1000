To stroke the little-g glyph:
Save the context.
Turn right.
Move 2 squares.
Turn left.
Move 1 square.
Turn left 3/8.
Stroke 1 square slantways.
Turn right.
Stroke 1 square slantways.
Turn right 1/8.
Stroke 1 square.
Turn right 1/8.
Stroke 1 square slantways.
Turn right.
Stroke 1 square slantways.
Reset the context.
Move 3 squares.
Turn right.
Move 2 squares.
Turn right.
Stroke 3 squares.
Turn right 1/8.
Stroke 1-1/2 square slantways.
Restore the context.
