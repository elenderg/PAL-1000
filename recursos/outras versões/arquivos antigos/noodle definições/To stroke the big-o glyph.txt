To stroke the big-o glyph:
Save the context.
Move 1 square.
Stroke 2 squares.
Turn right 1/8.
Stroke 1 square slantways.
Turn right 1/4.
Stroke 1 square slantways.
Turn right 1/8.
Stroke 2 squares.
Turn right 1/8.
Stroke 1 square slantways.
Turn right 1/4.
Stroke 1 square slantways.
Restore the context.
