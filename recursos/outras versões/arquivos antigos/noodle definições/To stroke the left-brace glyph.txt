Save the context.
Turn right.
Move 2 squares.
Turn around.
Stroke 1 square.
Turn right.

Turn left 1/8.
Stroke 1 square slantways.
Turn right.
Stroke 1 square slantways.
Turn left 1/8.
Stroke 1 square.
Turn right.
Stroke 1 square.
Restore the context.
