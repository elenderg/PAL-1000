To ajustar a picture (extrair caixas do gpbitmap):
If the picture is nil, exit.
Colocar 0 into the picture's box's left.
Colocar 0 into the picture's box's top.
Colocar the picture's gpbitmap's width minus 1 times the tpp into the picture's box's right.
Colocar the picture's gpbitmap's height minus 1 times the tpp into the picture's box's bottom.
Colocar the picture's box into the picture's uncropped box.
